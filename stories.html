Creation of the World

It all started when one deity in a different realm procrastinated and took a nap. The deity wasn't keen on continuing its task, so he went deep into the sleep. It accidentaly procrastinated further and took another nap within its nap.

When the deity transcended from one layer of unconsciousness into another layer, It damaged the barrier between two realms. Everything happened so suddenly - The two worlds were pulled to each other, creating massive amount of energy.

A huge electromagnetic field was formed, which led to immediate influx of radiation. Between two realms, instantaneously there was another type of void, but filled with an opposite attribute - presence of photons.

This sudden clash of infinite amount of opposite bodies, or in other words - darkness and light - created an enormous explosion. These sequence of actions in a linear form gave birth to what we call - time. The sparks were scattered everywhere, creating what we call - space.

Thinkers estimate, it has been 14 billion years since the explosion. Faithfulls believe the deity created light from the void. The whole event resembles the number 6. Depending on the perspective - it can be 9 too. But it's best to combine the both views.

This story does not fully explain what really happened. However, it is the closest interpretation. Maybe, we will learn the true history someday.
